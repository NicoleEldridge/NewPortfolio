Riverviews Magazine

Riverview Health magazine: "Riverviews". Images provided by the Riverview Health Marketing team and iStock. Laid out using Adobe InDesign.

Riverviews is a biannual magazine that offers health tips, inspiring medical stories, and Hospital updates to both employees and clients of Riverview Health. I work together with the Riverview Health Marketing team and 360 Group (an advertising agency) to create these magazines every Winter and Summer. First, the Marketing team provides me with the content and suggests what pages each article should be on. Then, I'll lay everything out and make sure that it all fits and is consistent with the magazine's set style. Finally, 360 Group reviews it and notifies the Marketing team and I if any further changes need to be made.

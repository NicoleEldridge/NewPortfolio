Wedding Collateral Set

Fantasy forest wedding collateral created using InDesign

Above are the invitations, thank you tags, escort cards, and more that I had designed for my wedding. The theme I chose was inspired by Macalania Woods from Final Fantasy X. I used a teal watercolor background to give replicate the glistening, blue-green moss that covers the ground in Macalania and used the black branches to create the forest feel and to make the teal background pop against the darker color. I used Palace Script ITC as my primary font because of it's elegant and romantic vibe and chose Eras Light to compliment it due to its delicate appearance.

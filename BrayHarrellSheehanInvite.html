Bray Harrell & Sheehan Invite

An invitation for the Bray, Harrell and Sheehan Society event hosted by Riverview Health. Created using Adobe Illustrator and InDesign.

This invitation was inspired the styles of the late 70's when the Beetles' White album (which is featured in the concert) was released. The ivory and red-orange rays bring a bright contrast to the design as well as a retro feel. Meanwhile the white clouds within the invitation give it a light and airy mood while also providing spaces for the text to be readable.

About Me

Hello! I'm Nicole Eldridge, a Graphic Designer located in Indianapolis, IN. I received my bachelor's degree in Graphic Design at Indiana State University in 2014 and have five years of experience working in the field.

I am employed by Integrity One Technologies as a Designer for one of their clients: Riverview Health. I am experienced with time management, page layout, marketing collaboration, and client communication. I am also proficient in Adobe Creative Cloud Suite and have experience using Wordpress. In addition, I am actively learning how to build websites using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.

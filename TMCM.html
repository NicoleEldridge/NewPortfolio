The Town Mouse & The Country Mouse Book

A storybook that I designed in College. Created using Adobe InDesign.

This book was a college project where I had to design an Aesop fable book using only fonts, basic shapes, and grayscale colors to illustrate the story. I used black, white, and a medium gray throughout the book to make up for the lack of color with a strong contrast of light and dark. I also used many different fonts to illustrate each word and bent the lines of text to make them a part of the illustrations on each page.

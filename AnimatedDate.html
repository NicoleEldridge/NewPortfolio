Animated Date

A Flash animation that I created for a digital art course in college. Created using Adobe Flash and After Effects.

In this project, I was given free rein to use any type of animation I wanted to create a short video. I chose to use all of the forms of animation that I had learned in class: stop-motion, rotoscope, and 2D. The story in this video is about me and my husband exploring different animation styles by becoming animated when a mysterious button shows up on our cell phones.

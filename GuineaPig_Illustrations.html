Guinea Pig Illustrations

Overview: For this project, I wanted to fill the blank wall above my guinea pig's cage with some artwork of my own.

Problem: I wanted to illustrate each of my guinea pigs, but I wanted the illustrations to look more modern and unique.

Solution: For each guinea pig portrait, I decided to zoom in close to get a more unique layout for each canvas and really let their markings define their faces. To create a more modern look, I simplified their features by focusing less on depth and more on their colors.
